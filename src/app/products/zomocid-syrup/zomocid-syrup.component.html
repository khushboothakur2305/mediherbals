ZOMOCID SYRUP

When Digestive System and Working disturbs them.

Effective in:

  • Dyspeptic Condition
  • Drug Induced Ulcers
  • Acute Gastritis
  • Stress Ulcers
  • Recurring Reflux Esophagitis
  • Recurrent Episodes of Peptic Ulcers

Dosage:

  • Adults: 2-3 Tsp 3 times a day
  • Children: 3-6 years: ½ tsp 2 times a day
    7-12 years: ½ tsp 2 times a day
Or as directed by Physician

Presentation:

  • 150ml
  • 450ml
