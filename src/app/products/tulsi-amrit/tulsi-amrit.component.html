TULSI AMRIT

Hot sip Kadha.
Immunity Booster

Benefits:

  • Useful in Enhance Immunity Ability
  • Relieve from cough, cold, Sore throat etc.
  • Prevent deformed phlegm in the body
  • Help to keep nose, gorge and health institution healthy

Preparation:

  • Take 200ml water (1 Glass Full) add 10g or (2tsp) Kadha.
  • Boil it for 10mins to make 1 cup of fresh extract.
  • Filter and Consume.
  • Small quantity of jiggery or munnaka can be added to impure the taste.

Dosage:

  • 2 times daily Or Directed by physician.
