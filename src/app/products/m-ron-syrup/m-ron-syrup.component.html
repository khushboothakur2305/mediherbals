M-Ron Syrup

Contributes to the normal formation of Hemoglobin and red blood cells.
Plus normal energy release and vitality with vitalizing herbs which contributes to the Reduction of Tiredness and fatigue.

Indications:-

  • Anemia Manifested by Generalized Weakness
  • General Debility
  • Oxidative Stress
  • Anorexia
  • Nutritional anemia

Dosage:

  • Syrup Adults: 3 tsp BD (men and women)
  • Children:
    3-6 years: 1-3 years 1/2tsp BD
    4-6 years 1 tsp BD
    7-12 years 2 tsp BD
Or as directed by Physician

Presentation:

  • 200ml
